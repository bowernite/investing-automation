Price

For Equities and ETFs, the price is the last trade price during the regular session. During the extended session, if the Last Trade price is outside the current bid and ask spread, or if there is no last trade price available, the bid price is used for long positions and the ask price is used for short positions. For Options, the mark price is used which represents the midpoint between the bid and the ask price. For Mutual Funds, the Net Asset Value (NAV) is used.

Gain/Loss

The "$ and % Unrealized Gain/Loss" is the hypothetical gain or loss that would be realized and potentially taxed if the investment was sold at the current market price. The calculation uses the current market value of the security minus its cost basis, (e.g., adjustments for any reinvestments, fees, and commissions). It is not intended to be used as a performance measurement, since it does not take into account all income generated by your investments.
